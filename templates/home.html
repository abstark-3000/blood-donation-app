Why Blood Donation Matters

Blood cannot be manufactured. It can only come from generous donors. Accident victims, cancer patients, mothers during childbirth, and people with chronic illnesses depend on timely blood donations.

Who Can Donate Blood?

Anyone aged 18–65, weighing over 50kg, and in good health can donate blood. The process is safe, quick, and supervised by medical professionals.

How Your Donation Helps

One unit of blood can save up to three lives. Your small act of kindness can give someone a second chance at life.

Benefits of Donating Blood

• Free basic health check-up
• Improves heart health
• Helps maintain healthy iron levels
• Gives a sense of purpose and social responsibility

Safe & Trusted Platform

We connect donors directly with verified hospitals and people in need. Hospitals can update urgent blood requirements in real time, ensuring your donation reaches where it is needed the most.
